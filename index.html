Roots & Proofs:

February 28, 2026 7 PM – 2 AM The House BK · Brooklyn, NY
About the Event

An immersive celebration of Black excellence through Black-owned spirits, music, storytelling, and community connection.

Hosted by Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., Gamma Iota Lambda Chapter at The House BK — 29 Locust St, Brooklyn, NY 11206 — Roots & Proofs: The Culture Amplified was a fundraising experience directly supporting youth mentorship, scholarships, and community programming across Brooklyn.

This wasn't just a party — it was heritage, storytelling, and culture poured into a glass. Brothers, friends, and community members came together for a night that honored who we are and where we come from.
